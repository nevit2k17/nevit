MORE

Easy Installation

No wall cutting or wiring is required. Simply mount the switch wherever is more convenient.

Batteryless

The switch operates on the kinetic energy added by your finger. There is virtually no maintenance once the switch has been paired up with the receiver.

Wireless

The switch utilizes a proprietary secure wireless protocol to communicate with the receiver. It is very easy to setup. 

Safe

All our products are RoHS compliant and FCC certified. The switch is also waterproof.

Control via an app

The receiver can be controlled via an app on your phone.

How it works

Step 1: Follow the instructions to connect the wires in the receiver
Step 2: Pair the switch with the receiver
Step 3: Mount the swtich and light fixtures
Step 4 (optional): Connect your phone with the receiver via zwave and our app. Note, you must have receiver model ERC307 .
